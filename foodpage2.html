Tomato Soup

Tomato soup is a comforting and classic soup made primarily from tomatoes. It can be served hot or cold, and variations exist with different textures and additions like cream, herbs, or other vegetables.

Potato Soup

Potato soup is a creamy and comforting soup made primarily from potatoes. It often includes other vegetables like onions, celery, and carrots, and can be thickened with cream, milk, or a roux. It's a versatile soup with many variations, and is often topped with cheese, bacon, or green onions.

Lettuce Soup

Lettuce soup is a surprisingly delicious soup made primarily from lettuce. It can be served hot or cold, and often includes other ingredients like broth, cream, and herbs. It's a great way to use up extra lettuce and can be a light and refreshing dish.

Minestrone Soup

Minestrone is a hearty Italian soup packed with vegetables, beans, and often pasta. It's a versatile dish with no strict recipe, so ingredients can vary depending on the season and what's available. It's typically a thick and flavorful soup, perfect for a comforting meal.

Manhattan Clam Chowder

Manhattan clam chowder is a distinct variation of clam chowder. Instead of a creamy base, it features a red, tomato-based broth. This gives it a lighter, tangier flavor compared to New England clam chowder. You'll find clams, potatoes, onions, and celery in it, and sometimes other vegetables.

Chicken Noodle Soup

Chicken noodle soup is a classic and comforting soup made with chicken broth, chicken pieces, noodles, and vegetables like carrots, celery, and onions. It's often used as a home remedy for colds and flu.
