Fruit Salad

Fruit salad is a refreshing dessert or side dish made with a mix of various chopped fruits. It can be dressed with juice, a light dressing, or simply enjoyed as is. The variety of fruits used can change with the season and personal preference.

Big Italian Salad

A big Italian salad is a hearty salad with mixed greens, a variety of vegetables (like tomatoes, cucumbers, onions, and peppers), olives, and often cheese and/or meat. It's typically dressed with a vinaigrette.

Smashed Cucumber Salad

Smashed cucumber salad features lightly crushed cucumbers, making them more flavorful and receptive to dressings. It's often dressed with a vinaigrette or an Asian-inspired dressing, and includes herbs like dill or mint.

Greek Salad

Greek salad is a simple and refreshing salad made with chopped tomatoes, cucumbers, red onion, and Kalamata olives. It's often topped with a block of feta cheese and dressed with olive oil, oregano, and sometimes a touch of red wine vinegar.

Pasta Salad

Italian pasta salad is a cold pasta salad typically made with small pasta shapes (like rotini or penne), a variety of chopped vegetables (such as bell peppers, red onion, olives, and sun-dried tomatoes), and often cheese (like mozzarella or provolone) and/or cured meats (like pepperoni or salami). It's typically dressed with an Italian vinaigrette.

Summer Salad

Summer salad with corn, strawberries, & avocado is a refreshing and vibrant salad that combines the sweetness of strawberries, the creaminess of avocado, and the crispness of corn. It often includes other ingredients like greens (spinach or mixed greens), red onion, and sometimes other fruits or vegetables. The dressing is usually a light vinaigrette, often with a hint of sweetness to complement the strawberries.
